CRIMINAL CASE PLEA AGREEMENT
Case No.: CR-2025-1234
United States v. Robert Johnson
PLEA AGREEMENT
The United States Attorney's Office and the defendant, Robert Johnson, hereby enter into this plea agreement pursuant to Rule 11 of the Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure.
Count 1: Conspiracy to commit wire fraud (18 U.S.C. ยง 1349)
Count 2: Money laundering (18 U.S.C. ยง 1956)
The defendant agrees to plead guilty to Count 1, conspiracy to commit wire fraud.
SENTENCING GUIDELINES:
The parties agree that the applicable guidelines range is 18-24 months imprisonment.
The defendant agrees to cooperate fully with the government's ongoing investigation.
The defendant agrees to pay restitution in the amount of $150,000 to the victims.
1. The defendant waives the right to appeal any sentence within the guidelines range.
2. The defendant agrees to forfeit all proceeds from the criminal activity.
3. The defendant will surrender all passports and agree not to travel outside the district.
This agreement is contingent upon the defendant's full compliance with all terms.
